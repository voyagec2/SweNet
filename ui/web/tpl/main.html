Introduction

XLore is to extract structured information from heterogenous cross-lingual online wikis and to share the extracted knowledge on the Web. To the best of our knowledge, XLore is the first large-scale knowledge graph with balanced quantity of Chinese-English knowledge. Currently, XLore contains 663,740 classes , 56,449 properties and 10,856,042 instances. It gives a new way for building a large-scale knowledge graph with balanced quantity of knowledge across any two different languages.
